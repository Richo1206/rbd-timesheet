RBD Travel Boundary

30-Minute Drive-Time Boundary · EA 2025
Visual guide only. Polygon shows approximate free-flow driving distances from Google Maps. Actual travel times vary with traffic, weather, and road conditions. Final 30-min Travel Boundary determinations are governed by the RBD Enterprise Agreement 2025 (Cl 64.1).
How it works. Pick a Centre of Employment (or type a Custom origin), choose a drive-time mode, and click Generate Boundary. The tool samples driving times in 100 directions out from the origin and draws: a solid dark-blue polygon for the primary boundary at your chosen drive time, and a thin red outline showing the raw, unsmoothed samples for that boundary. In 30-min mode it also draws a lighter blue halo at drive time + 7 min, the effective EA boundary accounting for Cl 53.b rounding. In 20-min mode only the primary polygon is drawn, since the Inc-1 threshold isn't subject to the rounding rule.
