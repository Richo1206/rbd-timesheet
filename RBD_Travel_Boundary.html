RBD Travel Boundary

30-Minute Drive-Time Boundary · EA 2025
Visual guide only. Polygon shows approximate free-flow driving distances from Google Maps. Actual travel times vary with traffic, weather, and road conditions. Final 30-min Travel Boundary determinations are governed by the RBD Enterprise Agreement 2025 (Cl 64.1).
How it works. Pick a Centre of Employment (or type a Custom origin), set the drive time, and click Generate Boundary. The tool samples driving times in 100 directions out from the origin and draws a polygon of the reachable area. The polygon is smoothed (median + mean filter, plus curve-fitted edges) to remove road-snapping artefacts: sharp inward dips that happen when one bearing snaps to a fast highway and the next to a back street. Tick Advanced → Show raw polygon to compare against the unsmoothed shape.
